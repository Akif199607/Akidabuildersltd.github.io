AKIDA BUILDERS LIMITED

A TRUSTWORTHY DEVELOPER

Akida Builders Ltd. started its journey in 2019 in the real-estate sector with it's first project namely "AKIDA SAIKAT" at Bashundhara R/A Akida Builders Ltd. is also involved in businesses like Akida Fisheries & Agro, Akida Events & Akida IT Solution. Our Managing Director Md. Zakir Hossain Bhuiyan has a long track record of construction since 2000, having been involved in various projects of its own. He has been involved in constructing many mega structures of Dhaka city in various prime locations. Eventually to pursue the passion for his own construction, Akida Builders Ltd. was established in 2016 with a mission to change the lifestyle of city dwellers by providing modern, functional and aesthetic living and working spaces that can only be compared to the most successful developers of the globe. With the belief that construction is not just about building structures - but an Art, SHL goes beyond the traditional scopes of property development and integrates the best the world has to offer. Since then, Akida Builders Ltd. has been working on to be one of the most reputed and fastest growing real estate developer of the country.
